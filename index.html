About Us

At Flavours of Home, we bring the taste of tradition to your table. From handmade Momos to the hearty Thakali Khana, each recipe reflects a story of heritage and love.

Nepal is a land of diverse cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and, most importantly, flavourful dishes that tell the stories of its people. At Flavours of Home, we’re dedicated to sharing the heart and soul of Nepali cuisine. From the vibrant spices of our curries to the delicate flavours of traditional dumplings, we bring you the authentic tastes that define Nepal.

Recipe Book

Momo

Steamed dumplings filled with flavorful meat or veggies, served with spicy chutney.

Ingredients: Flour, ground meat/veggies, garlic, ginger, spices.
Steps: Make dough, prepare filling, shape dumplings, steam, and serve with chutney.

Thakali Khana

A traditional platter of rice, lentils, and curries, served with pickles and greens.

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, vegetables, spices, pickles.
Steps: Cook rice and lentils, prepare curries and pickles, and serve on a platter.
